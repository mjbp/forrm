1. Default installation

HTML5 validation with inline errors straight out of the box

//Create an HTML5 form

  <div class="forrm-row">
    <label for="search-field">Search*</label>
    <input type="search" id="search-field" name="search-field" placeholder="Your search terms..." value="" aria-required="true" required>
  </div>
  <input type="submit" value="Search">
</form>

4. Steps

Break a single form down into steps. In this example a simple form is split into three steps, styling hooks added to the form element itself power the step indicator.

<form id="example-steps-1" action="/">
  <div class="forrm-step-list">
    <span class="forrm-step-list--1">Step 1</span>
    <span class="forrm-step-list--2">Step 2</span>
    <span class="forrm-step-list--3">Step 3</span>
  </div>
  <fieldset data-forrm-step="1">
    <div class="forrm-row">
      <label for="example-steps-1-1">What is your name? *</label>
      <input type="text" id="example-steps-1-1" name="example-steps-1-1" value="" placeholder="Arthur, King of the Britons" required aria-required="true">
    </div>
  </fieldset>
  <fieldset data-forrm-step="2">
    <div class="forrm-row forrm-row-checkbox">
      <fieldset>
        <legend>What is your quest</legend>
          <div class="forrm-row forrm-row-radio">
            <input type="radio" id="example-steps-1-2" name="example-steps-1-group" value="holy-grail" required aria-required="true">
            <label for="example-steps-1-2">To seek the holy grail</label>
          </div>
          <div class="forrm-row forrm-row-radio">
            <input type="radio" id="example-steps-1-3" name="example-steps-1-group" value="nothing" required aria-required="true">
            <label for="example-steps-1-3">Nothing in particular</label>
          </div>
        </fieldset>
      </div>
    </fieldset>
    <fieldset data-forrm-step="3">
      <div class="forrm-row">
        <label for="example-steps-1-4">What is the air-speed velocity of an unladen swallow? *</label>
        <input type="text" id="example-steps-1-4" name="example-steps-1-4" value="" placeholder="An African or European swallow?" required aria-required="true">
      </div>
    <input type="submit" value="Submit">
  </fieldset>
</form>

<script>Forrm.init('#example-steps-1');

6. Custom validation constraints

Define custom constraint functions, pass them to Forrm in the constructor and attach them declaratively to elements. In this example the value of both fields must first pass the usual constraint validation then must also be the same value as each other.

7. Custom fail and success functions

Pass custom functions to Forrm in the constructor to be executed on validation fail, or on validation success instead of form submission.

Conditional

Enable a disabled field or part of a form based on a previous field. The defaut conditional constraint is for the anticedent field to have a non-null value. This exmaple accept a custom condtional constraint enabling the consequent field if the value of the group equals 'other'.
